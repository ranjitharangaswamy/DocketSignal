Cross-discipline claim

Legal reasoning shapes the UX research question

DocketSignal frames public legal-AI discourse as user research for legal-tech product decisions. The legal side helps identify what matters: citation trust, confidentiality, professional responsibility, billing incentives, sanctions risk, and governance inside firms. The UX side turns that discourse into coded patterns, evidence panels, memos, and product implications that a stakeholder can inspect.

This is where the project fits my background. Legal training pushes me to ask what supports a claim. UX research pushes me to ask who needs the claim, what decision it informs, and how quickly someone can check the evidence. DocketSignal is built around that overlap.
